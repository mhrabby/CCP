[GMAT]


The Graduate Management Admission Test is a computer adaptive test that is primarily used to secure admissions in graduate programs. The test assesses one’s skills in analytical, quantitative, verbal and writing skills; that is to say it judges one’s skills in three primary categories: English, Math and Critical Reasoning. The GMAT and the GRE are both used when it applying to graduate programs, but the GMAT is better for applying to STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics).
