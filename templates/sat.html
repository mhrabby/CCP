[SAT]


The SAT or Standardized Aptitude Test is a universally recognized test for college admissions. (This is done so since the US does not have any standardized examinations at school levels). The SAT is exclusive to the US and students seeking admission to American universities gain a tremendous advantage in securing their desired universities and gaining scholarships with a high SAT score. High scores in the SAT also exempt students from needing to appear in local university admissions exams. This is due to the fact that university admissions exams in Bangladesh are designed after the SAT.
